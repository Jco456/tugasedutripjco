The Wonderful City of Surakarta

When I arrived at the city, it felt like a burning flame. The museum of Rumah Atsiri that got a tremendous amount of scents with thousands of spices and fresh smells. But eventually, eating the foods that have been prepared by the restaurant goes different from my expectation. The chicken is so juicy, because the crust and the most felt chewy and also flavorful. The food in this city feels like eating in heaven.

Experiencing

The atmosphere in Surakarta is lighten me up to try the culture around the city. It proofs me wrong that the city isn't full of thieves and also people with mental disorder around the city. Sangiran museum filled with historical story and get me interested with the topics and also the miniature inside the museum. My overall experience in Surakarta is very exciting and memorable. The trip to Surakarta is very beneficial for my experience learning about culture.
